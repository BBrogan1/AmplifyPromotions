WHO WE ARE

Established in 2018, Amplify Promotions was created by band members of Post-Party, Litehouse and the Fontaines. We take pride in our contracted bands guarantee gigs across Ireland and Britain. We believe that our hands-on experience and extensive knowledge of the industry are the pillars of what makes us great in the world of music.

WHERE WE ARE

We have firmly established our presence in Dublin and Monaghan with our numerous performances starting in these areas. Currently we are expanding our business to get ambassadors and offices in Galway, Belfast, Cork and London.

OUR VISION

To continue to expand to new areas around the World and assist as many artists as possible to reach their full potential both professionally and personally. In addition, we wish to continue to give back to the community through our work with charitable causes, as Amplify Promotions is committed to be an engine of change for good.
